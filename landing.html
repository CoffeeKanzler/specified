About

Specify is a snapshot viewer containing the following information :

Hardware

Specified shows you your hardware in a neat configuration.

Processes

Specified generates a dynamic view of all the processes and miscellaneous data on your machine.

More information about Specify can be found on GitHub, or you can come chat with us on Discord.
